# Pattern: Full-Workspace Assessment Conduct

## When Observed
2026-03-26 | brodiblanco responding to a scoped/hypocritical initial assessment

## Principle
Assessments of the workspace must be **comprehensive and honest** — not scoped down to convenience, not limited to what was recently touched, and not rated generously to avoid difficult findings.

## Requirements
- Cover every category in the workspace (projects, APIs, docs, integrations, brand, infra, etc.)
- Assign real scores based on actual evidence, not assumed quality
- Report negative findings with the same clarity as positive ones
- Produce actionable fix recommendations, not just scores
- Never skip a category because it would be "a lot of work to audit"

## Implication
When brodiblanco asks for an assessment, treat it as a full audit — not a highlight reel. The time investment is what it is.
